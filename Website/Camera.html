Camera

The Camera is the little lens found in front and back of youre smartphone, its purpose is to capture beautiful and quality picutures or videos in just a tap. In addition, camera quality has become so good that, except for the professional photographer or serious amateur, the average family no longer uses a dedicated camera. Although the test of camera quality has always been printing large photos, even 8x10" prints from many camera phones are extremely good.

How does a camera work?

The main process of a camera to capture an image is when the user focuses the lens and light enters, its then when the aperture determines the amount of light that reaches the sensor and the shutter determines how long the sensor is exposed to light, the sensor then captures the image, hardware processes and records the image and can be saved into the users gallery or software repository.
